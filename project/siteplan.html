Nigeria Travel and Culture Guide

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Nigeria Travel and Culture Guide

This name was selected because it clearly represents the purpose of the website, which is to provide information about Nigeria's tourist attractions, culture, food, and major cities.

Optional Domain: nigeriatravelguide.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate visitors about Nigeria by providing information about tourist destinations, traditional foods, culture, and major cities. The website will serve as a simple guide for people who want to learn more about Nigeria.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular tourist attractions in Nigeria?
  • What traditional foods are commonly eaten in Nigeria?
  • Which cities should I visit when traveling to Nigeria?
  • What cultural traditions make Nigeria unique?

Color Schema

Green #008751

Green will be used for headings, navigation, buttons, and important highlights.

Gold #D4AF37

Gold will be used for accents, call-to-action elements, and special sections.

White #FFFFFF

White will be used as the primary background color.

Typography

Poppins

Poppins will be used for headings and section titles.

Arial

Arial will be used for body text and general content.
